Pitch Report

Executive Summary:

Moon Delivery is a startup that offers a convenient and hassle-free way for customers to order food, groceries, and e-commerce products online and get them delivered to their doorstep. Our service offers free and fast delivery, and we use only organic vegetables for our food products. We will be starting 20 restaurants in Visakhapatnam, and orders will be sent to the nearest restaurant for faster delivery and customers will receive their food within 15 minutes. In addition to food delivery, we also offer e-commerce and grocery delivery, and our customers can expect to receive their grocery delivery in 15 minutes and e commerce delivery in 15 to 25 minutes.

Market Opportunity:

The food and grocery delivery market has seen a significant increase in demand due to changing lifestyles and the COVID-19 pandemic. According to a report by Statista, the online food delivery market in India is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% from 2021-2025, reaching a market volume of US$ 7,521m by 2025. The grocery delivery market is also expected to grow at a CAGR of 22.6% from 2021-2026, reaching a market volume of US$ 1,018m by 2026. With these market trends in mind, Moon Delivery sees an opportunity to tap into this growing market and provide a unique offering with our focus on organic vegetables and fast delivery.

Product and Service Offering:

Moon Delivery offers food delivery, grocery delivery, and e-commerce delivery services to customers in Visakhapatnam. Our food delivery service includes a wide variety of dishes made with organic vegetables and offers free and fast delivery. Our grocery delivery service is available for a range of products and is priced at MRP without any additional fees. Our e-commerce delivery service will be offered through our websiteand will be delivered to customers' doorsteps in the shortest possible time.

Business Model:

Moon Delivery's business model is based on delivering high-quality products and services at affordable prices, with free delivery as a key differentiator. Our primary revenue stream comes from charging a commission on each transaction completed through our app, website, or call center. We also generate revenue from partnerships with e- commerce and grocery partners, which pay us a commission on sales. Our cost structure is primarily fixed, with expenses incurred for maintaining our own stores, managing our fleet of delivery personnel, and investing in technology and marketing.

Marketing and Sales:

Moon Delivery will leverage a variety of marketing and sales channels to attract and retain customers in Visakhapatnam. We will use social media and online advertising to generate awareness and attract new customers, while our user-friendly app and website will provide a seamless ordering experience. We will also offer various incentives and rewards to encourage customer loyalty, such as discounts on future purchases and referral bonuses.

Financial Projections:

Based on our market research and estimated costs, we anticipate generating a revenue of INR 5 crore in the first year of operations, with a profit margin of 15%. Our projected revenue is based on an average order value of INR 500 in groceries, e-commerce and INR 100 in food, with an average commission rate of 20%. We plan to break even within 2 years and aim to expand our operations to other cities in India.

Conclusion:

Moon Delivery is committed to providing quality, convenience, and affordability to customers in Visakhapatnam. Our free delivery service, wide range of products and services, and user-friendly app and website will set us apart from the competition. With a focus on organic vegetables, fast delivery times, and 24/7 availability, we are well- positioned to become a leading player in the fast-growing food and grocery delivery market.
